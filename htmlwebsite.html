Grilled Cheese Recipe

You will need:

  1. Bread VERY IMPORTANT
  2. Cheddar Cheese (sliced)
  3. Mozzarella Cheese (shredded)
  4. Ground Black Pepper
  5. Paprika
  6. Garlic Powder
  7. Human souls
  8. Air fryer
  9. 20 minutes of spare time

Instructions

  1. First of all, if your bread looks like what are you even doing. Go get better bread. Sourdough is best, but if you're on a budget I would recommend this tasty french bread (or https://www.walmart.com/ip/Freshness-Guaranteed-French-Bread-14-oz/46491756?classType=REGULAR&athbdg=L1200&from=/search if the link doesn't work).

  2. Place two slices of bread facedown in the air fryer and heat 375 degrees Farenheit for 2-3 minutes. The bread should be a bit crispy when finished.

  3. Next, flip over each slice of bread and place 1 slice of cheddar and a handful of mozzarella on each slice (amount varies depending on the size of the bread slices.) Heat at 375 for 3 minutes, enough to melt the cheese. The cheese side must be face-up!

  4. Third, place a few pinches of paprika, pepper and garlic powder (ideally in that order) on top of the cheese, then place slices of bread on top. It should look like a sandwich now. Heat at 375 for 3 more minutes.

  5. Eat!
